    Celsius Conversion Formula

    Celsius to Fahrenheit °F = °C × 9/5 + 32
    Celsius to Kelvin K = °C + 273.15
    Celsius to Rankine °R = °C × 9/5 + 491.67
    Celsius to Réaumur °Ré = °C × 4/5

    Celsius: The most commonly used temperature scale worldwide.

    °F

    Fahrenheit: Commonly used in the United States.

    K

    Kelvin: The international (SI) unit. 0K is "absolute zero".
